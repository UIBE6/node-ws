保护莽山烙铁头

Protect the Mangshan Pit Viper

莽山烙铁头（Protobothrops mangshanensis）是全球最稀有的毒蛇之一， 仅分布于中国湖南莽山国家级自然保护区。

The Mangshan Pit Viper (Protobothrops mangshanensis) is one of the rarest venomous snakes in the world, found only in Mangshan National Nature Reserve, China.

物种科普

Species Overview

基本特征

Key Characteristics

莽山烙铁头是中国特有的极危物种，体型大、颜色独特， 分布范围极其狭窄，是典型的旗舰物种。

This species is endemic to China, known for its large body size, unique coloration, and extremely limited distribution.

保护意义

Conservation Significance

其数量稀少、对栖息地高度敏感， 是研究亚热带森林生态系统与生物多样性保护的重要对象。

Due to its small population and habitat sensitivity, the species plays an important role in biodiversity conservation research.

保护行动

Conservation Actions

栖息地保护

Habitat Protection

保护莽山亚热带森林生态系统， 是维持该物种生存的基础条件。

Preserving the subtropical forest ecosystem of Mangshan is essential for the survival of the species.

科学监测

Scientific Monitoring

通过野外调查、红外相机和数据分析， 研究其种群动态与生态功能。

Field surveys, camera traps, and data analysis are used to study population dynamics and ecological roles.

公众科普

Public Education

通过科学传播减少误解， 促进人与野生动物的理性共存。

Science communication helps reduce fear and misinformation and promotes coexistence between humans and wildlife.
